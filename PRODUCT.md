# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want a fast, local-first place to capture notes, lists, drafts, formulas, and reference material across desktop and mobile screen sizes.

## Product Purpose

This app recreates the core Simplenote workflow as a browser app: write quickly, find notes instantly, organize with tags and pins, preview Markdown, recover history, and move notes in or out through import and export.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, exact, dependable. The UI should stay out of the way and make repeated note-taking feel calm and efficient.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, decorative card grids, oversized hero typography, novelty controls, heavy gradients, glass effects, and any layout that makes writing feel secondary to decoration.

## Design Principles

- Put writing first.
- Make search and tags immediate.
- Keep controls familiar and predictable.
- Preserve user content with local persistence and history.
- Adapt structurally across desktop, tablet, and phone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target keyboard-first use, visible focus states, readable contrast, reduced-motion support, 44px touch targets on mobile, and responsive layouts that avoid text overlap.
